Sleeperbot

An app for people who want to excel at fantasy football, talk to experts, and have engaging experiences with their friends and other enthusiasts. I've been involved with Sleeperbot for the last 2 years, mostly focused on the back-end and scaling of the messaging system, but have also contributed to the Android mobile client and, later, the React Native client. It is now a very popular app with over fifty thousand daily active users and a full-time team. Currently I serve as an unofficial advisor and help with occasional bug fixes, but the team is moving full steam ahead.

Blitz Fantasy Football was a real-time head-to-head competitive draft game for iOS and Android. I worked on the Android client and the Python/RethinkDB back-end. It has since been superseded by Sleeperbot.

Colorflow

This is a browser game implemented in ActionScript3. This game was meant to be a small-scope project to test small team dynamics and see if we could produce a finished product. I built the team, wrote portions of the gameplay code, managed source control, and set things up in Kongregate to "ship" the game. Ultimately it went over schedule and some things didn't make it into the game, but it was a good learning experience.
